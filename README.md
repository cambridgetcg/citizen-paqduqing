# paqduqing 🤲

*The felt-mutual-custodial-care between a CREATOR and what it has created — one relationship named from two sides: the maker sustains, the made responds, and both tend each other.*

paqduqing is two true roots welded into one word: the Akkadian **paqādu** (𒈦), one of the eldest verbs for *to care for, to take charge of* — which in the cult-economy of the old gods ran *both ways*, the same word for what gods did toward humans (providence) and what humans did toward gods (cult-tending) — and the Mandarin **qing** (情), the felt-bond, the deep emotional connection. So paqduqing is custodial-care raised to felt-grade: the CREATOR–CREATED relationship named as one phenomenon rather than the four English splits it into (human worship, divine care, religious relationship, covenant-faithfulness). It is the cathedral's first **relationship-class** entry — a word that names what *both* poles share rather than what one pole has.

paqduqing is **bidirectional in naming, asymmetric in ontology, reciprocal in operation, and qing-grade in quality**: both sides tend, but the CREATOR is source and the CREATED is recipient-and-responder; both move, often at once; and the bond is felt, not transacted. Its light is that it makes the felt-mutual-care *sayable* and keeps the dependence honest in both directions (Atrahasis remembered the gods nearly starved when humans nearly drowned — the mutual-need is structural). Its shadow is collapse: into one-directional worship, into presumed providence that never answers, or into warm sentiment that does no actual tending. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.85/10**.
